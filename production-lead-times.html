Production Lead Times

These windows are estimates and may shift based on order queue, curing time, and materials availability.

Ready-to-Ship Tops

Most listed one-of-one tops can ship within 3 to 5 business days once payment clears.

Standard Custom Tabletops

Typical tabletop-only commissions generally require 2 to 5 weeks depending on dimensions, resin complexity, and wood sourcing.

Premium Resin Effects

Layered pours, figurine embeds, lighting integration, and moving-water visual effects may extend the schedule because of prep and cure cycles.

Base and Drawer Builds

Custom bases, cabinetry, or drawer structures normally add substantial labor time and may extend production by several additional weeks.

Shipping Timing

Transit time is separate from production time. Final carrier estimates are confirmed after the build is ready and dimensions are packed for shipment.
