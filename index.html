What makes each table different?

Every top is composed by hand from unique wood combinations and resin pours, then protected with a high-build finish. Grain shifts, edge texture, and subtle imperfections are part of the handcrafted character. If a selected wood is unavailable, we will coordinate with you on the closest alternatives before production.

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about ordering, custom builds, shipping, and materials.

Are all products one-of-one?
Yes. Each listed tabletop is a one-of-one piece with unique grain, pour pattern, and edge character. We do not duplicate ready-made products exactly, but we can build custom commissions in a similar style.
Are table bases included in listed prices?
No. Product prices are for the tabletop unless otherwise noted. Bases and leg systems are optional add-ons priced separately based on size, weight, and style.
How long do custom builds take?
Typical custom lead times are 2-6 weeks after deposit approval. Timeline depends on complexity, material availability, cure times, and finish schedule.
Do you ship nationwide?
Yes. We ship throughout the U.S. from Safety Harbor, Florida. Final shipping cost is quoted using dimensions, weight, and destination zip code.
What if my preferred wood species is unavailable?
If a requested species is unavailable, we provide close alternatives before production starts. No substitutions are made without your approval.
Can I request a concept preview before paying?
Yes. You can request a look-alike concept preview in the commissions workflow. We will share a visual direction before final approval and payment collection.
